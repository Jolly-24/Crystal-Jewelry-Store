About Us

our mission

We do fine jewelry differently.

We believe in jewelry as an expression of self, and how you show up in the world should have no limits. That’s why we work with master jewelers to create pieces that feel like you — to mean what you want, wear how you want, and keep forever. Our jewelry is made to live in, designed in-house with craftsmanship and responsible sourcing at the cornerstone of it all.

Commitments

EMPOWERMENT FUND

we’ve partnered with various corporate giving partners to provide women and non-binary people with broader financial and educational resources to empower themselves and their future.

QUALITY&CRAFTSHIP

We remove the middlemen and work directly with the best jeweles around the world. Our pieces are made to last using high_quality,responsibly sourced materials

SUSTAINABILITY

To create true sustainability in fine jewelry we need to be able to trace the impact of our pieces from the origin of the materials all the way to their final destination: you.
